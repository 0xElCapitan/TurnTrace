## Deliverable-intent guard (run_eval)

A ledger row is written ONLY with explicit deliverable intent (`--deliverable` or
`--ledger PATH`); a bare invocation writes `summary.csv` but **no** ledger row
(`eval/run_eval.py:335`). `--no-ledger` is deprecated (no-write is now default).
